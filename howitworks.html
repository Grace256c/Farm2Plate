Smarter Farms.
Better Harvests.

No internet. No smartphone. Just a basic phone and a SIM card.

Planting Calendars

AI-generated planting schedules based on crop, district, and rainfall patterns.

Pest & Disease Alerts

Early warning SMS when outbreaks are detected in nearby farms.

Live Market Prices

Real-time commodity prices from Owino, Nakawa, and Gulu markets.

02
🛒 Digital Marketplace

Direct Sales.
Zero Middlemen.

Farmers list their produce directly on the platform. Buyers — households, schools, restaurants, and NGOs — browse and order. Surplus food is automatically matched to vulnerable families and donation partners.

Direct Listings

Farmers list produce via SMS: "LIST TOMATO 50KG 1800 LUWERO" — no smartphone needed.

AI Surplus Matching

Unsold food is automatically matched to NGOs, food banks, and vulnerable households within 24 hours.

Fair Pricing Engine

AI analyses market trends to suggest fair prices, preventing exploitation of farmers.

Donation Portal

NGOs and individuals can donate money to purchase surplus food for families in need.

AI Route Optimisation

Real-Time Tracking

Farmers and buyers receive SMS updates at each stage of the delivery journey.

04
🏫 School Feeding Link

Fresh Food.
Brighter Futures.

Schools place standing orders directly from verified local farmers — bypassing expensive middlemen and ensuring children receive fresher, more nutritious meals. The platform handles scheduling, payment, and delivery coordination automatically.

Standing Orders

Schools set weekly or monthly food orders. Farmers get predictable, reliable income.

Nutrition Standards

Each meal plan is reviewed against WHO child nutrition guidelines by our health team.

NGO Subsidy Integration

WFP and UNICEF funds automatically subsidise meals for schools in food-insecure districts.

Nutrition Reporting

Schools receive monthly nutrition dashboards showing meal quality and child health trends.

05
💳 Mobile Money & Finance

Instant Payments.
Microfinance Access.

Every transaction on the platform flows through MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money — the two networks covering 95% of Uganda. Farmers get paid instantly upon delivery confirmation, with no bank account required.

Instant Settlements

Farmers receive payment within 60 seconds of delivery confirmation via MTN MoMo or Airtel Money.

Savings Wallets

Farmers can open a Farm2Plate savings account and earn 8% annual interest on stored earnings.

Microloans

Farmers with 3+ months on the platform can access loans of UGX 200,000–2,000,000 for seeds and inputs.

Crop Insurance

Parametric weather insurance automatically pays out when rainfall falls below threshold levels.

Tailored SMS tips for pregnant and breastfeeding mothers on iron, folate, and protein-rich local foods.

Recipe Suggestions

Weekly recipes using seasonal, locally available, affordable ingredients from the Farm2Plate market.

Health Alerts

Community-level disease outbreak warnings and hygiene reminders during high-risk seasons.
