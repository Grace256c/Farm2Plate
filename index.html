⚙️ The System

How It Works

Six integrated modules working together to solve food insecurity from farm to plate.

🌾
01

Smart Farming Advisory

Farmers receive SMS + voice messages with weather forecasts, planting schedules, pest control advice, and market prices — boosting productivity.

SMSVoice AIWeather API
🛒
02

Digital Market & Donations

Farmers list produce directly. Surplus food is automatically matched to vulnerable households, schools, and NGOs — reducing waste and hunger simultaneously.

MarketplaceAI MatchingDonations
🚛
03

Transport & Logistics

Connects farmers with boda bodas, trucks, and logistics partners. AI optimizes delivery routes for lowest cost and fastest time.

Route AIBoda BodaTracking
🏫
04

School Feeding Link

Schools place orders directly from local farmers, ensuring children receive fresh, nutritious meals while farmers gain reliable buyers.

School OrdersNutritionLocal Sourcing
💳
05

Mobile Money & Finance

Seamless payments via MTN MoMo & Airtel Money. Microfinance tools offer savings accounts and small loans to farmers and households.

MTN MoMoAirtelMicroloans
🥗
06

Nutrition & Health AI

Weekly SMS/AI nutrition tips tailored to household needs. Focus on child health, pregnancy, and balanced diets using locally available foods.

AI TipsChild HealthSMS

🧠 Our Approach

Why It Works

Root Causes, Not Symptoms

We fix broken distribution, not just deliver aid. Sustainable systems outlast one-time interventions.

Inclusive SMS Technology

No smartphone needed. Our SMS-first design reaches the last mile — even in areas with no data coverage.

Evidence-Based Design

Every feature is grounded in Uganda-specific research, community input, and nutritional science.

Self-Sustaining Ecosystem

Market fees, microfinance margins, and partnerships fund ongoing operations without perpetual donor dependence.

Radical Transparency

Every donation tracked on-chain. Farmers see prices. Donors see impact. Nothing hidden.

African-Centered Design

Built with local communities, in local languages, respecting local food cultures and trade patterns.
